About Me

The Eye
Behind
The Frame

I am Ige Dominion, a multi-disciplinary visual artist based in Abuja, Nigeria. My passion lies in telling powerful stories through cinematography, motion design, and digital visuals — turning raw ideas into breathtaking experiences that connect, inspire, and endure.

From directing cinematic shoots to crafting immersive 3D animations and razor-sharp VFX, I bring a complete creative vision to every single project. I have trained professionals, mentored students, and collaborated with brands and media organisations across Nigeria.

Whether it's a broadcast promo, a brand film, an animated sequence, or a week-long masterclass — the standard is always the same: extraordinary.

"Every frame is a statement. Every edit is an emotion. Every animation is a world."
